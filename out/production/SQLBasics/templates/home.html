Hello, {{name}}. Submit your favorite restaurants and their attributes to add them to your List of Restaurants and press Submit Restaurant.

If you would like to delete a restaurant entirely, enter the number of the restaurant from your List of Restaurants below in the first box.
Then reenter all of the attributes you had used to create the restaurant in the next 3 fields, and press Delete Restaurant.
Note: Do not enter the id # in the first box. Only enter the # from the List of Restaurants #s. (They might be the same #)
You are only allowed to edit a restaurant's name. If you would like to do so, enter that restaurant's attribute's again in the first row of boxes.
Then type in the exact same # in the first box, then the new restaurant's name, and reenter all of the previously used attributes, in the next row of boxes.
Note: Do not enter the id # in the first box. Only enter the # from the List of Restaurants #s.
Your updated restaurant will be added at the bottom of your Official List of Restaurants, but the id # will remain the same.
